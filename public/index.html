About

A brief introduction

I'm Somuel Tripura — a developer who discovered that writing is just as important as coding. I spend my mornings with coffee and a text editor, my afternoons building things for the web, and my evenings reading books that make me think differently.

This journal is where I explore the intersection of technology, language, and personal growth. I believe that learning to write clearly is learning to think clearly — and I'm still learning both.

I write about building software with care, learning English as a second language through stories instead of rules, and the quiet philosophy of living with intention in a noisy world.
